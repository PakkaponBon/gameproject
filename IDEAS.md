## Rejected (with reason — stop re-discussing)
- Multiplayer — engineering cost incompatible with solo scope
- Z-levels — map complexity explosion
- Full RimWorld-style medical/organ sim — wrong depth for this game's combat model
- Infinite/procedural world — one home map + abstract world map is a pillar
- Naval anything — no.
- Real-time simulated enemy settlements — that's a 5-year different game; expeditions auto-resolve
