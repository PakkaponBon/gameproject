# ROADMAP-2.2.md — The Ordered Village  *(colony-management depth)*

> Post-2.1 systems pass. Low-risk by design: reads/extends existing state,
> no new atlas cells (art-safe alongside Codex), additive saves, minimal new
> UI. Makes the village feel less opaque and the new content actually flow to
> the villagers who need it.

## Phase QC1 — Mood Transparency  *(villager card)* ✓
- [x] The card shows a short "thoughts" list — why mood is up or down
      (hungry / exhausted / cold / restless / warm by the fire / somewhere
      pleasant / at ease). Reads existing needs + world state; no new data.

## Phase QC2 — Weapon Upgrades  *(job system)* ✓
- [x] Villagers now upgrade melee arms: an unarmed OR weaker-armed villager
      claims a better melee weapon on the ground and drops the old one for
      the next fighter. So the LR4 weapon spread (and fallen warhammers)
      actually reaches the people who should carry them. Ranged/skill-gated
      weapons keep their existing unarmed-only rule.

## Phase QC3 — A Full Table  *(food seeking)* ✓
- [x] Villagers who aren't starving prefer cooked food (meals/stew) over raw
      when both are reachable — so the stove's mood/joy payoff actually lands
      instead of being wasted on someone eating a raw turnip next to a feast.

## Phase QC4 — Ship it
- [ ] DoD (human): read a villager's mood, watch arms trade up to the best
      wielder, see cooked food get eaten first. Then fold into the next tag.
